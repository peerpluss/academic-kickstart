## Quill&Scroll简介

美国高中传媒竞赛，每年有近20万美国高中生参与，
是美国中学校长协会(National Association of Secondary School Principals)官方推荐的竞赛活，
受到全美各个院校的认可。The National Association of Secondary School Principals (NASSP) is the leading organization of and voice for principals and other school leaders across the United States.
Since 1941, NASSP has published the NASSP List of Approved Contests, Programs, and Activities for Students.
